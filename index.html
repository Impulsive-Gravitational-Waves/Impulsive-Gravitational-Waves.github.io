Impulsive gravitational waves are exact solutions to Einstein equations with curvature proportional to Dirac $\delta$-distribution localized on some null hypersurface. Solution corresponds to pure gravitational waves if the corresponding components of Weyl tensor are proportional to $\delta$-distribution. This page is meant to be a summary of current results concerning impulsive gravitational waves.
