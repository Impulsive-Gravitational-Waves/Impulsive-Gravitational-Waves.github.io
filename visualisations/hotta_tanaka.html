Hotta-Tanaka Solution

Hotta-Tanaka is an exact solution corresponding to ultraboost of Schwarzschild-(anti-)de Sitter spacetime. It is a $\Lambda \neq 0$ analogue to Aichelburg-Sexl solution. In de Sitter case, this spacetime represents non expanding spherical wave generated by two particles moving apart from each other. In anti-de Sitter case, the impulsive hypersurface has hyperboloidal geometry and is generated by a single null particle moving back and forth in between spatial infinities.
